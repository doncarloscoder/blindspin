How it works

Simple as vinyl

01

🎵

Spin

One player spins the virtual record. It lands on a song — nobody knows which one yet.

02

📷

Scan

The other players scan the QR code on the label. The music starts playing — no title, no artist.

03

🎧

Listen

Everyone listens. Recognise the melody? The singer? Place it on your timeline.

04

✦

Reveal

Flip the card. See the year, the title, the artist — and who wrote it. Score your points.

◆ ◆ ◆

Two roles

One table, two phones

💿

The DJ

Generator

Spin the turntable. A random song is selected and its QR code appears on the record label. You're as blind as everyone else.

📷

The Listener

Player

Point your camera at the label. The music plays instantly — no hints, no title, just sound. Trust your ears.
