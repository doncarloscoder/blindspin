How it works

Simple as vinyl

01

🎵

Spin

Flick the record. It picks a random song from your playlist. Nobody knows which one yet.

02

🎧

Listen

The music plays with the screen face-down. No title, no artist, no hints. Just sound.

03

🤔

Guess

Everyone places the song on their timeline. Trust your ears. Argue with each other.

04

✦

Reveal

Tap to reveal the year, the title, the artist — and who actually wrote the song.
